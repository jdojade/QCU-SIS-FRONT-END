PROCEDURE FOR REQUESTING SOA

    Here are the procedures on how to request a STATEMENT OF ACCOUNT:

  1. Proceed to the Admin Building, 2nd Floor then go to the Accounting Office.
  2. Go to the available window and ask the accounting staff that you're requesting for a statement of enrolled for the semester
  3. The Accounting Staff will as for your official registration form or school id to verify if you're currently enrolled for the semester.
  4. Once the Accounting Staff verified your student number on their system, the staff will return your registration form/ school id.

    5. DISCLAIMER: THEY DO NOT ASK FOR PHOTOCOPY OF REGISTRATION FORM AND SCHOOL I.D. JUST PRESENT TO THEM YOU'RE REGISTRATION FORM OR SCHOOL ID FOR VERIFICATION TO THEIR SYSTEM

  5. Lastly, Just wait for 3-5 minutes to process and print your statement of account.
